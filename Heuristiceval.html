Heuristic Evaluation

What is an Heuristic Evaluation?

A usability expert or even an individual with knowledge on usability principles can assess a system/website using a set of well known heuristics. A heuristic evaluation looks for possible usability problems within the user interface.

There are many different heuristics that can be used when conducting a heuristic evalutaion but most likely Nielsens Heuristics are commonly used due to them being well known and the ten heuristics cover usability issues.

Benefits of Conducting A Heuristic Evaluation

  • Allows you to identify and address problems in early stages of development
  • General test users may not be able to spot usability problems that a usability expert is capable of
  • Experts are not required to go through a script and set of tasks like users therefore able to quickly evaluate a product
  • Experts can detect errors within the whole interface compared to users who view only high priority parts of the product
  • Saves time and money from having to redesign a finished product
  • Easy, Fast and Cheap to conduct heuristic Evalutaions
  • Good method for identifiying major and minor usability problems
  • Assigning the correct heuristic can help suggest the best corrective measures to designers
